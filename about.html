Biography

Patrick S. Phelan, MD, MPHS, FAAD
- B.A. Psychology (Phi Beta Kappa), University of Arkansas '14
- M.P.H.S. Clinical Epidemiology, Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis '20
- M.D., Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis '20
- Residency in Dermatology, University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences '24

I am a board-certified dermatologist and academic physician in Arkansas. I specialize in complex medical dermatology in both the outpatient and inpatient settings, and have expertise in hidradenitis suppurativa, autoimmune blistering diseases, and more. I am particularly passionate about patient education, quality improvement, therapeutic risk management. Above all, the goal of preventing and relieving suffering is central to my philosophy of medicine, and guides every recommendation I make for my patients.

Outside of dermatology, my clinical interests include general and preventive medicine, infectious disease, and nutrition and metabolism. My scholarly interests include methodology in evidence-based medicine and biostatistics, clinical reasoning, medical ethics, and medical education.



I judge my scientific alignment to be between Chaotic Neutral and Chaotic Good.
