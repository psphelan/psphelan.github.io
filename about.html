Biography

Patrick S. Phelan, M.D., M.P.H.S.
- B.A. Psychology (Phi Beta Kappa), University of Arkansas '14
- M.P.H.S. Clinical Epidemiology, Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis '20
- M.D., Washington University School of Medicine in St. Louis '20
- Residency in Dermatology, University of Arkansas for Medical Sciences '24

I am a freshly trained dermatologist at Ozark Dermatology, where I am fortunate to practice general dermatology and Mohs micrographic surgery at our Fayetteville, Siloam Springs, and Springdale (Highlands Oncology) locations.

Outside of dermatology, my clinical interests in include general and preventive medicine, infectious disease, and nutrition and metabolism. My scholarly interests include methodology in evidence-based medicine and biostatistics, clinical reasoning, medical ethics, and medical education. This biography will be expanded eventually.

I judge my scientific alignment to be between Chaotic Neutral and Chaotic Good.
